FEEDING & SWALLOW THERAPY GUIDELINES

RECOMMENDED GOALS

Oral Motor Skills
- Improve lip closure
- Improve tongue mobility
- Improve jaw stability
- Improve chewing skills
- Improve oral coordination

Feeding Skills
- Improve acceptance of food textures
- Improve food variety
- Improve self-feeding skills
- Improve utensil usage
- Improve meal participation

Swallowing Skills
- Improve safe swallowing
- Improve bolus control
- Improve swallowing coordination
- Reduce choking risk
- Improve feeding efficiency

Sensory Feeding Skills
- Improve tolerance to food textures
- Improve tolerance to food smells
- Improve tolerance to food appearance
- Reduce food aversion
- Improve sensory regulation during meals

SUGGESTED CLINICAL ACTIVITIES

Oral Motor Activities
- Lip strengthening exercises
- Tongue exercises
- Jaw strengthening activities
- Blowing activities
- Straw drinking activities

Feeding Activities
- Texture progression activities
- Food exploration tasks
- Self-feeding practice
- Spoon and fork training
- Structured mealtime activities

Sensory Feeding Activities
- Food play activities
- Sensory food exploration
- Texture tolerance activities
- Smell and taste exposure activities

Swallow Activities
- Safe swallowing exercises
- Controlled chewing activities
- Oral preparation exercises

HOME PROGRAM

Daily (15-20 Minutes)

- Oral motor exercises
- Structured mealtime routine
- Food exploration activities
- Self-feeding practice
- Introduce new foods gradually
- Encourage positive feeding experiences

EXPECTED OUTCOMES

- Improved oral motor control
- Improved chewing skills
- Improved swallowing safety
- Increased food variety
- Improved feeding independence
- Reduced feeding difficulties
