HOME PROGRAM

Daily (15-20 Minutes)

- Complete one structured tabletop activity
- Follow 2-3 step instructions

- Letter identification practice
- Number recognition activities
- Matching and sorting games

Communication Skills
- Read one story daily
- Ask WH questions
- Encourage conversation

Fine Motor Skills

- Play dough exercises

Independence Skills

- Put away toys independently
- Practice dressing and self-care tasks
