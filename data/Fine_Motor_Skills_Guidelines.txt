FINE MOTOR SKILLS GUIDELINES

RECOMMENDED GOALS
- Improve hand strength
- Improve finger strength
- Improve grasp patterns
- Improve pincer grasp
- Improve tripod grasp
- Improve bilateral coordination
- Improve dexterity
- Improve in-hand manipulation
- Improve visual-motor integration
- Improve hand-eye coordination
- Improve pre-writing readiness
- Improve functional independence

- Bead stringing

- Cloth peg activities

- Play dough activities
- Putty exercises
- Tweezers activities
- Puzzle completion
- Coin insertion activities
- Buttoning and zipping
- Cutting activities
- Sponge squeezing
- Nuts and bolts activities
