HANDWRITING GUIDELINES

RECOMMENDED GOALS
- Improve pencil grasp
- Improve hand stability
- Improve writing posture
- Improve letter formation
- Improve letter sizing
- Improve alignment
- Improve spacing
- Improve writing speed
- Improve writing endurance
- Improve writing legibility
- Improve visual-motor integration
- Improve copying skills

SUGGESTED ACTIVITIES
- Pre-writing strokes
- Tracing activities
- Shape drawing
- Sand writing
- Air writing
- Vertical surface writing
- Pattern copying
- Maze activities
- Pencil control exercises
- Highlighted line worksheets
- Copying tasks
- Timed writing activities

WEEKLY SCHEDULE
Monday: Pencil grasp and pre-writing strokes
Tuesday: Letter formation practice
Wednesday: Spacing and alignment activities
Thursday: Copying and sentence writing
Friday: Functional classroom writing tasks

HOME PROGRAM
Daily 15 Minutes
- Tracing practice
- Coloring within boundaries
- Maze activities
- Copying simple words
- Vertical surface writing
- Drawing and coloring activities
- Short handwriting worksheet
